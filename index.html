WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW BEFORE APPLYING

We want every applicant to understand the criteria, commitments, and expectations attached to ILM-SA bursary funding.

Zakaah funding

Funding context & fairness

Most of the funding we receive for this bursary comes in the form of zakaah. Zakaah funds must be allocated to specific categories of Muslims. We therefore prioritise Muslim students who qualify for zakaah support; however, all eligible students—Muslim and non-Muslim—are welcome to apply.

Bottom line All qualifying students may apply, but zakaah-eligible Muslim applicants receive first consideration.
Deadline

Application cut-off

Applications close sooner once 100 complete submissions are received.

Final deadline Sunday, 18 January 2026

Eligibility checklist

Make sure you satisfy every requirement before you begin your application.

  • You are an undergraduate student (no funding for postgraduate studies).
  • You are a South African citizen.
  • You reside in KwaZulu Natal. You may study elsewhere if the programme is unavailable locally, but KZN residency is required.

During your bursary year

You’re expected to:

  • Volunteer at ILM-SA events throughout the academic year.
  • Attend mentorship sessions and development programmes arranged by the organisation.
  • Once you are employed, contribute a monthly donation back to ILM-SA, calculated according to your financial situation.

Media & visibility

We regularly publish respectful photographs and videos featuring bursary students on our platforms. Apply only if you and your guardians are comfortable with this level of visibility.

Competition & next steps

Funding is limited and demand is high. We cannot guarantee support for every applicant, so continue applying for other bursaries and backup options in parallel.

Four-step process

How to apply

Follow each step sequentially. Every action builds on the last to keep your application on track.

  1. 01
    Step 01

    Follow sequentially

    Launch the online application form

    Select “Apply” on this site and carefully work through each question. Give yourself at least 20 minutes, double-check your information, and be truthful throughout.

    Remember: You must complete every required field before you can submit.
  2. 02
    Step 02

    Follow sequentially

    Watch for your confirmation email

    Within minutes you’ll receive a confirmation email from ILM-SA. It details the supporting documents we need and how to prepare them.

    Remember: Read the email in full and keep it handy—everything else happens via that thread.
  3. 03
    Step 03

    Follow sequentially

    Reply with your supporting documents

    Gather the requested documents, ensure they are clear, and reply directly to the confirmation email with all attachments. Keep the subject line unchanged.

    Remember: Send one reply with complete attachments instead of multiple partial emails.
  4. 04
    Step 04

    Follow sequentially

    Await shortlist updates & interviews

    If shortlisted, we’ll contact you by Monday, 26 January 2026 to arrange an interview. No news by then means your application was not successful this time.

    Remember: Keep your contact details up to date and monitor your inbox (and spam) regularly.
TIP

Prepare clear digital copies of your documents before you start—the review team can progress your application faster when everything arrives together.
